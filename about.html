About Me

Background

Born and raised in Gorakhpur, Uttar Pradesh, India. I hold an MSc in High Performance Computing from EPCC, University of Edinburgh, and a BSc (Hons) in Computer Science from Robert Gordon University, Aberdeen, Scotland (2022).

Professional Experience

I currently work as an HPC Software Engineer at Calligo Technologies, Bengaluru. My work here sits at the intersection of computational physics, numerical methods, and low-level systems engineering — implementing, profiling, and comparing parallel solvers across conventional x86 (IEEE 754) and the Uttunga RISC-V board, which features experimental posit arithmetic support. Projects so far:

  • Multi-material thermal analysis (Poisson equation, Hypre BoomerAMG): Implemented a 2D thermal solver for a composite CFRP + aluminium domain using algebraic multigrid. Ported and profiled on the Uttunga board; visualised with GLVIS and ParaView.
  • OpenMP-parallel Gauss-Seidel smoother in OpenFOAM: Introduced shared-memory thread-level parallelism into a production CFD codebase using a Red-Black two-stage ordering scheme. Deep profiling across multiple grid resolutions using AMD uProf, Intel VTune, Intel Advisor, and LIKWID — covering cache behaviour (hits, misses, prefetches), memory bandwidth, roofline analysis, and power consumption.
  • Custom LBM CFD kernel: Built a Lattice Boltzmann Method kernel from scratch, extending a textbook implementation with a full physical validation suite: energy decay, mass conservation, L∞ norm, zero-velocity test, and more. Serial and OpenMP-parallel variants, in both double and single precision. All versions ported to Uttunga and benchmarked against x86 baselines.

All projects are accompanied by technical reports covering implementation, profiling results, and cross-architecture numerical comparisons between IEEE 754 and posit arithmetic.

Previously, I worked as a freelance developer for hospitals in Gorakhpur, India, building electronic patient record and staff management systems. I also worked at Fennex, Aberdeen as a full-stack web developer (ASP.NET, TypeScript, React, SQL), and contributed heavily to the UI design of their flagship BBSS product on iOS and Android.

Outside of professional work, I build and publish software independently — usually tools that solve problems in my own life:

  • Mandi Mitra (iOS & Android) — A grocery price calculator for traditional Indian farmers' markets, with unit conversion and a native app update checker that works independently of Apple/Google services.
  • QuickPanchang (macOS) — A Hindu calendar and festival app with multi-language support, macOS integration, and custom-built web scrapers, APIs, and caching proxy servers.
  • API Comparator Tool — A request/response comparison tool for analysing API behaviour across versions.
